Prototype Anglo Concertina Fingering Generator

Caveats:

  1. Only Jeffries button layouts supported right now.
  2. No chords, only single melody lines are assumed.
  3. ABC with embedded quotes not supported yet.
  4. Push/Draw bellows aren't indicated.
  5. Button notation assumes a 30 button concertina.
  6. Not all key signatures are supported yet.
  7. Button generation is currently arbitrary!!! Fixed soon!!!
  8. Accidentals are not carried to other unmarked notes in the same measure.

Button notation:The top (accidental) rows are numbered L1-L5 on the left side, with L1 being the leftmost button, and R1-R5 on the right side with R1 being the leftmost button. Likewise, L6-L10 and R6-R10 are the middle row buttons, and L11-R15 and R11-R15 are the bottom row buttons.

After pasting in an ABC tune, copy the result into your clipboard and use it on many of the fine PDF generator sites for ABC notation. My personal favorite is Draw The Dots.
